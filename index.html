Christina's Resume

I'll preface this by saying that nothing on this page is not already available on the internet. If you have stumbled across this page and you are not a recruiter, well, here's my story. In chronological order, you'll see some key highlights that bring out my experience and who I am in a more consumable way. The intent is to give a better glimpse as to who I am, in ways that a plain old resume could never convey.

2012

In 2012, Christina graduated with honors from Gulliver Preparatory School in Miami, FL, where she served as captain and starting pitcher of the women's softball team for three years. That year, the team achieved a historic "three-peat," winning the Florida 3A state finals for three consecutive years and making headlines across the state.

She received a full scholarship to attend George Mason University, being honored both with athletic and academic scholarships.

Image 2012

2013

In 2013, the George Mason University (GMU) softball team welcomed Tara Sankner as part of their partnership with the Friends of Jaclyn Foundation (FOJ). Christina played a pivotal role in establishing "Go Gold 4 Tara," acting as the primary event planner and content creator for the event.

The choice of gold was symbolic, representing both the ribbon color for childhood cancer awareness and a school color at GMU, making it a fitting tribute to honor Tara as she joined her new team.

For more information on Tara's adpption, visit the Friends of Jaclyn Foundation.
