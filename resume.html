2013

In 2013, the George Mason University (GMU) softball team welcomed Tara Sankner as part of their partnership with the Friends of Jaclyn Foundation (FOJ).


For more information on Tara's adoption, visit the Friends of Jaclyn Foundation.


Over the summer, Christina returned home to lead week-long softball camps for her high school alma mater, Gulliver. Managing upwards of 40 girls aged 8 to 18, she designed engaging programs that focused on skill development, teamwork, and sportsmanship. This experience not only allowed her to share her passion for the game but also honed her leadership and organizational skills, as she navigated the challenges of coordinating activities and fostering a positive environment for young athletes.

2012

In 2012, Christina graduated with honors from Gulliver Preparatory School in Miami, FL, where she served as captain and starting pitcher of the women's softball team for three years. That year, the team achieved a historic "three-peat," winning the Florida 3A state finals for three consecutive years and making headlines across the state.
She received a full scholarship to attend George Mason University, being honored both with athletic and academic scholarships.
